To Find the Third Angle of a Triangle:

Using the inputted values for angle 1 and angle 2, we can calculate the value of the third angle by summing the two angles and subtracting it from the total of all angles, which is 180 degress.

Formula: Third Angle = 180 - (angle 1 + angle 2)
